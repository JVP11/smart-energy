Entity-Relationship Overview

8 tables with referential integrity. Primary keys (PK), foreign keys (FK), and constraints.

admins (PK: id) ──┬── power_reports (FK: admin_id) users (PK: id) ─────┬── power_reports (FK: user_id) ├── usage (FK: user_id) ──► appliances (PK: id) ├── bills (FK: user_id) └── facilities (FK: user_id) tariffs (PK: id) — standalone (rate per kWh)

Normalization

  • 3NF — Tables are in Third Normal Form (no transitive dependencies)
  • Referential integrity — Foreign keys enforce valid references
  • UNIQUE constraints — email (users), username (admins)
