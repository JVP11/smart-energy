📋 What is NEC Load Calculation?

NEC = National Electrical Code (USA). Load calculation = estimating how much power your building draws so you can correctly size wires, breakers, and panels.

  • Connected load — Sum of all appliance power (W)
  • Demand load — Realistic peak (connected × demand factor)
  • Demand factor — Not everything runs together (e.g. 0.8 = 80%)
  • Panel size — Breaker rating in Amps for safety
