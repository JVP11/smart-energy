Enterprise Power & Energy Management

Unified platform combining NEC-compliant load sizing, grid infrastructure tracking, consumption modeling, and real-time facility monitoring. Built for facilities, utilities, and energy managers.

📌 How to use
  1. Register or log in to access all features
  2. Go to Load Calculation to size your panel (single/2/3 phase)
  3. Add appliances & usage in Dashboard to estimate bills
  4. Open Kerala Power Map to report outages or view infrastructure
📐 Load Calculation Used for: NEC panel sizing, wire gauge, breaker rating. Enter connected load and get recommended amps. 🔌 Infrastructure Used for: Grid topology, facilities, Kerala map. Add buildings/zones and link to power map. 📊 Energy Modeling Used for: Bill estimates, appliance comparison, usage planner. Compare costs and save energy. 🏢 Facility Management Used for: Tracking buildings, zones, outage reports. Manage locations and view reports.
